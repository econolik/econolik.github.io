🏛 About the Course

Economics is the science of how people make decisions under conditions of scarcity, and how those decisions shape entire markets, countries, and the global economy. Why do prices rise? Why do some countries grow rich and others don't? What happens when a government prints money? These questions affect all of us — regardless of profession or age.

The course is built around N. Mankiw's "Principles of Economics" — one of the most popular introductory courses in the world. Mankiw wrote it for beginners: no heavy math, but a genuine understanding of how economic life works. The same topics — in an interactive format with quizzes, terms, and simulations.

The material fully aligns with the IEO (International Economics Olympiad) curriculum — so if you're preparing for the olympiad, this course covers the theoretical foundation.

📚 Course Sections

📖 Theory — 10 topics from basic principles to macroeconomics. Read in any order; each topic is marked as read.
⚡ Blitz Quizzes — short quizzes per topic (4–5 questions). A topic is passed at ≥ 80%. Retakes allowed — best result is saved.
🃏 Terms — match concepts to definitions. Trains fast term recognition; best time is recorded.
🎮 Games — cocktail stand simulator. Practice managing costs, pricing, and profit. Results saved to profile.

⭐ How Points & Achievements Work

Read a topic — +10 pts
Correct answer in quiz — +10 pts base. With a streak: +streak×2 pts. E.g., 4 in a row = 10 + 4×2 = 18 pts
Pass quiz (≥80%) first time — +25 pts bonus (once only)
Complete terms — 10–30 pts depending on errors
Profile rank updates automatically by % completion per section: 0% → 🔴 beginner, 1–39% → 🟠 learning, 40–69% → 🟡 getting it, 70–99% → 🟢 confident, 100% → 🏆 master
